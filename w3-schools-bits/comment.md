## Comments
    Comments are text that is ignored upon execution, it can be used to explain your code and also make it more readable.

    Go supports 2 lines of comments which are the single-line comments and the multi-line comments.

    Single-line comments starts with two forward slashes  //, and any text after the two forward slashes will be ignored by the compiler and will not be executed.  **While**

    Multi-line comments starts with /* and ends with */,

    and any text between /* and */ will be ignored by the compiler and will not be executed.
